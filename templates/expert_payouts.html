Payment Help

How do payouts work?

Your earnings from sessions are automatically transferred to your connected bank account via Stripe Connect. Payouts are processed weekly when your balance reaches $10.00 or more.

When will I receive my money?

Payouts typically take 2-3 business days to appear in your bank account after processing. The exact timing depends on your bank's processing schedule.

Need help with payments?

Contact our support team at support@droply.live for assistance with payment issues.
